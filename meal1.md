# Quinoa, Sauteed Cauliflower and Lentil Curry
## Ingredients
- Quinoa: 1 bag?
- Cauliflower: 2lbs?
- Lentils: 1 bag?
- Carrots: 2lbs?
- Yellow Onions: 2
- Celery: 4 Stalks
- Red Kale: 1lb?

## Preparation
- Quinoa in Rice Cooker
- Cauliflower sauteed with oil and salt
- Chop Carrots, Onions, celery and kale
- Lentils and water in slow cooker
- Slow cook high for 4 hours
